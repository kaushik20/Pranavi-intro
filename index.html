About Sri Pranavi Chintalapudi

Her journey in Kuchipudi and beyond

Sri Pranavi Chintalapudi began her Kuchipudi training at the age of 8 under the guidance of Smt. Preethi Tatambhotla. Over the past 11 years, she has performed at esteemed venues like Nadasneerajanam (Tirumala), Guruvayurappa Temple (Kerala), and Chidambaram Temple (Tamil Nadu).

A versatile artist, she has been learning Carnatic vocal music since age 5 under Smt. Gayatri Sriram and Smt. Poorima Rao, and is currently under Smt. Prema Iyer. She also studies Carnatic violin and Sanskrit, and is pursuing an engineering degree at BITS Pilani, Dubai Campus.

She is also pursuing a Diploma at the University of Silicon Andhra.
