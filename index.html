Sri Pranavi Chintalapudi

Rangapravesam | August 2025

Sri Pranavi Chintalapudi is a gifted Kuchipudi dancer, training for over 8 years under Smt. Preethi Tatambhotla. She has performed at renowned venues across India and the UAE — from Tirumala and Guruvayur to Chidambaram and international stages.

She’s won 1st Prize in the Virtual International Competition (Spring Nectar Foundation, USA), Shobha Naidu Awards, and bagged a Gold Medal at the Indywood Talent Hunt International 2021. She’s also taken center stage in lead roles of ballets choreographed by Tanmayi Art Studio.

Academically brilliant, she’s currently studying Engineering at BITS Pilani, Dubai Campus, after graduating from GEMS Our Own English High School.

Her talents extend to Carnatic vocal music (under Smt. Gayatri Sriram, Smt. Poornima Rao, Smt. Prema Iyer) and Carnatic violin under Mr. Subramaniyam. She has won awards at Margazhi Utsavam, Sangeet Samrat, and Vivek 157.

A language enthusiast, she’s also learning Sanskrit through Samskritha Bharathi Institute LLC and aced Pravesha & Parichaya Patralaya exams with First Class distinction.

We warmly invite you to witness her Rangapravesam — a divine milestone of grace, rhythm, and passion.
