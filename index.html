About Pranavi

Her journey in Kuchipudi

Pranavi began her Kuchipudi training 8 years ago under the tutelage of Guru Smt. Preethi Tatambhotla. Over the years, she has graced cultural and devotional stages across India and the UAE, including iconic venues like Tirumala, Guruvayur, and Chidambaram. Her dedication to classical dance is a blend of discipline, devotion, and sheer passion.
