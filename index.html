About

Pranavi Chintalapudi is a passionate Kuchipudi dancer trained under Smt. Preethi Tatambhotla. With performances at revered venues such as Tirumala, Guruvayur, Chidambaram, and international stages, she has upheld the elegance of classical Indian dance. She is a recipient of top awards including the 1st Prize in the Spring Nectar International Competition and the Indywood Talent Hunt Gold Medal. She's currently pursuing Engineering at BITS Pilani, Dubai Campus.
