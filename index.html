About Priya

Her journey in Kuchipudi

Priya began her Bharatanatyam training at the age of 6 under the guidance of Guru Smt. Lakshmi Iyer. Her passion for dance has grown over 10 years, culminating in this Arangetram, a testament to her discipline and artistry. Bharatanatyam, with its intricate footwork and expressive storytelling, has shaped her into a dedicated performer.
